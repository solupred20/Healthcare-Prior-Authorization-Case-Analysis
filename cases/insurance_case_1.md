# Insurance Case 1: Prior Authorization Review

## Patient Information
- Age: 60
- Gender: Male
- Diagnosis: Type 2 Diabetes Mellitus
- ICD-10 Code: E11.9

---

## Requested Service
- Medication: Insulin Glargine
- CPT/HCPCS Code: J1815

---

## Clinical Background
The patient has been treated with Metformin and Glipizide for over 6 months without adequate glycemic control.

HbA1c remains above 9%.

---

## Insurance Policy Criteria
According to policy:
- Insulin therapy is approved if oral medications fail
- Documentation of HbA1c > 8% is required
- Step therapy must be completed

---

## Review Decision
Approved

---

## Rationale
- Documented failure of oral therapy
- HbA1c above threshold
- Meets step therapy requirements
- Aligns with insurance policy

---

## Notes
This case represents a standard approval scenario based on payer guidelines and clinical necessity.
