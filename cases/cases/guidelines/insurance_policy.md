# Insurance Policy Guidelines (Sample)

## General Principles

- All requests must meet medical necessity criteria
- Step therapy is required when applicable
- Documentation must support diagnosis and treatment need

---

## Medication Approval

Approved if:
- First-line therapy has failed
- Clinical evidence supports escalation
- Patient condition requires advanced therapy

---

## Imaging Approval

Approved if:
- Red flag symptoms are present
- Conservative treatment has failed
- Clinical justification is documented

---

## Denial Conditions

- Insufficient documentation
- Request is premature
- Not aligned with policy guidelines
