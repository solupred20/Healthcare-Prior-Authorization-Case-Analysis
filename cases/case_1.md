# Case 1: Prior Authorization Request

## Patient Information
- Age: 55
- Gender: Male
- Diagnosis: Type 2 Diabetes Mellitus
- Condition: Poorly controlled blood glucose levels

---

## Requested Service
- Medication: Insulin Glargine
- Type: Long-acting insulin

---

## Clinical Background
The patient has been on oral antidiabetic medications (Metformin and Sulfonylurea) but failed to achieve adequate glycemic control.

HbA1c remains above target levels.

---

## Medical Necessity Justification
Insulin therapy is recommended when oral medications are insufficient.

Long-acting insulin helps maintain baseline glucose control and reduce complications.

---

## Decision
Approved

---

## Rationale
- Failure of first-line therapy
- Clinical guidelines support insulin initiation
- Risk of complications if untreated

---

## Notes
This case demonstrates a standard approval scenario based on clinical necessity and guideline adherence.
