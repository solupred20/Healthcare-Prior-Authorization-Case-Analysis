# Case 2: Prior Authorization Request

## Patient Information
- Age: 40
- Gender: Female
- Diagnosis: Mild Lower Back Pain

---

## Requested Service
- Procedure: MRI Scan (Lumbar Spine)

---

## Clinical Background
The patient reports mild lower back pain for one week. No history of trauma, neurological deficits, or red flag symptoms.

No prior conservative treatment has been attempted (e.g., physical therapy, NSAIDs).

---

## Medical Necessity Review
According to clinical guidelines, imaging is not indicated in the early stages of uncomplicated lower back pain unless red flags are present.

---

## Decision
Denied

---

## Rationale
- No evidence of severe or progressive neurological symptoms
- No prior conservative management attempted
- MRI not medically necessary at this stage

---

## Recommendation
- Initiate conservative treatment (NSAIDs, physical therapy)
- Reassess if symptoms persist or worsen

---

## Notes
This case demonstrates appropriate denial based on evidence-based clinical guidelines and utilization management principles.
