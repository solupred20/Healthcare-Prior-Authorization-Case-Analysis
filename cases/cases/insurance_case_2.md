# Insurance Case 2: Prior Authorization Review

## Patient Information
- Age: 35
- Gender: Female
- Diagnosis: Migraine
- ICD-10 Code: G43.909

---

## Requested Service
- Procedure: MRI Brain
- CPT Code: 70553

---

## Clinical Background
Patient reports mild recurrent headaches. No neurological deficits, no red flag symptoms.

No prior treatment with preventive or acute migraine therapy.

---

## Insurance Policy Criteria
MRI is approved only if:
- Neurological deficits are present
- Severe or worsening symptoms
- Failure of conservative treatment

---

## Review Decision
Denied

---

## Rationale
- No red flag symptoms
- No prior treatment attempted
- Does not meet medical necessity criteria
- Does not meet insurance policy requirements

---

## Recommendation
- Initiate migraine management (analgesics, preventive therapy)
- Reassess if symptoms worsen
